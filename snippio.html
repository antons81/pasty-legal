01

Information We Collect

Snippio is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide the app's functionality.

  • No personal information — We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information
  • No usage analytics — We do not track how you use the app, which features you access, or how often you open it
  • No crash reporting — We rely on Apple's built-in crash reporting tools only
💡 Snippio operates entirely on your device. Your code snippets and templates are yours and stay on your device (or your iCloud account for Pro users).
02

Data Storage

All data created in Snippio is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • Local storage — Your snippets, preferences, and app data are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage APIs
  • iCloud sync (Pro only) — If you subscribe to Snippio Pro, your snippets are synced via iCloud using CloudKit. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • We have no access — We cannot access, read, or modify your iCloud data. It is encrypted and accessible only by you
03

Purchases & Subscriptions

Snippio offers optional in-app purchases including monthly, annual, and lifetime Pro subscriptions.

  • All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store any payment information
  • Purchase history is managed by your Apple ID
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in System Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Refund requests should be directed to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com
04

Third-Party Services

Snippio uses only Apple's first-party frameworks and services:

  • SwiftData — Local data persistence
  • CloudKit — iCloud sync for Pro subscribers (Apple's service)
  • StoreKit 2 — In-app purchases (Apple's payment system)
✅ We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third parties.
05

Children's Privacy

Snippio is designed for developers and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no personal information from any users, this applies equally to all age groups.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Snippio after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

07

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete on our end. All your data (snippets, preferences) can be deleted by simply uninstalling the app from your Mac.

  • To delete local data — uninstall Snippio from your Mac
  • To delete iCloud data — go to System Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage and remove Snippio data
